Custard

Ingredients

  • 500ml milk
  • two large eggs
  • a fuck ton of honey
  • a fuck less of vanilla essense

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a large bowl and add vanilla.
  2. Bring milk and honey to a boil and reduce by 50ml.
  3. While beating the eggs like crazy stir in the hot milk slowly.
  4. If it doesn't look custardy, put the mixture back in the pan and bring the heat up slowly while stir continuously. DON'T BOIL!
