Moka Coffee

A Simple Way

  1. Get some water boiling in a kettle.
  2. Grind that coffee up, not too coarse and not too fine.
  3. Pour the boiled water in the bottom part of the moka pot and put the grinds in the second level.
  4. Twist the top on and place it on some slow heat.
  5. When the coffee begins streaming out, take care it doesn't gush.
  6. If it gushes, take it off the heat for a moment.
  7. At the first sign of spurting, run the bottom pat under cold water.
  8. Drink strong or add some hot water to dilute a little.
